Frequently Asked Questions

How do I set up my natal chart?

After signing in, enter your name, date of birth, time of birth (if known), and birth city. Tap "Calculate My Chart" and your personalized natal chart will be generated instantly.

What if I don't know my birth time?

You can check "I don't know my birth time" during setup. Your Sun sign and most planetary positions will still be accurate, but your Rising sign and house placements may not be available.

How do I manage my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID. Go to Settings on your iPhone, tap your name, then Subscriptions to view, change, or cancel your Aligno Premium subscription.

How do I delete my account?

Contact us at the email below to request account deletion. All your data will be permanently removed within 30 days.
